EXPERIENCES

Going through the 4 years of experiences in KCMIT, I find myself grateful to be a part of KCMIT and always be. We have the best groups of lectures and everybody is so cooperative with all the students. All the college representatives and administrative staffs are extremely friendly and helpful. Here each student are supported mentally and physically and the most encouraging factors is the endearing word from our Respected Principle Ma’am ‘My Child”. She is an inspiration to everyone. For me KCMIT is the best platform for every student to learn many things which will help to achieve everything one dreams of. Lastly, studying at KCMIT was the best part of my life. I miss my college life at KCMIT very badly.

Aashika Dahal

BBA 2010-2014
