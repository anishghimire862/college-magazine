Anish Ghimire

She, an Angel!!

untold stories,
hidden scars,
pain behind her smile.
tried to kill herself,
still alive,
maybe god has something more to give.
she, an angel.
princess of her dad.
unfortunately,
he thought her a bitch.
used her body,
ditched her
and she is now a slut.
twinkling stars,
she stares at,
tears fall from her eyes.
nobody to hold her hand,
he is at the bar.
perplexed you,
still in remorse.
how can i make you understand,
unrequited love kills?
smile suits you,
better than those tears.
now, hide your pain.
conceal your scars.
show him your real worth.
dont feel so cold,
be bold.
its time to move on
alot more is yet to come.
