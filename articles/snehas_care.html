Sneha's Care

Sneha's Care is a government-registered nonprofit organization. It focuses on rescuing street dogs and
nurturing them in the best possible manner. To make it simple, Sneha's Care has turned into an animal 
shelter from November 2015. The organization rescues injured street dogs and keeps them in its shelter 
until they recover. It does not matter what type of disease they have, they give their best to cure them. 
Their objective is to get the community involved as much as possible and raise awareness in animal welfare
in the hope of changing the general attitude of people towards street dogs. In a short period of time, 
this organization has been able to rescue most of the dogs and animals from streets and has taken care of 
them under the personal guidance of the founder,Ms.Sneha Shrestha. Sneha's Care has been taking care of 
neglected and rejected dogs and giving them a warm home.
